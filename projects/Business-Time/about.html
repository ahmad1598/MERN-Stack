​Heydar was born and raised in Iran and has been passionate about art since 2008. He enjoys all different types of art such as visual art, music, and theater. He graduated from Tehran Fine Art School ​in 2012 for painting. He has participated in a number of painting events and workshops and was recognized as one of the top 100 young artists in a drawing festival in Iran in 2012. Heydar also enjoys working with kids of different ages to engage their creativity and improve their painting skills and he has a lot of experience as a youth workshop instructor. Heydar came to Utah and is currently working hard to adapt to his new life in the Unites States. He enjoys all that Utah has to offer including its beautiful landscapes and its great people.
